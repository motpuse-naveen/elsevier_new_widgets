EXERCISES

The exercises that follow are designed to help you learn the terms presented in the chapter. Writing terms over and over again is a good way to study this new language. As you check each answer, you not only will reinforce your understanding of a term but often will gain additional information from the answer.

Each exercise is designed not as a test, but rather as an opportunity for you to learn the material.
