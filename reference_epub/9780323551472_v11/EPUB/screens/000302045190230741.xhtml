CKD Stages

The five stages of CKD reflect increasing severity of kidney disease:

Stage 1: eGFR >90
Stage 2: eGFR 60-90
Stage 3: eGFR 30-60
Stage 4: eGFR 15-30
Stage 5: eGFR <15
