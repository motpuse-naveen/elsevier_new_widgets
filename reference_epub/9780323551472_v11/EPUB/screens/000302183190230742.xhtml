Is Botox Cosmetic Safe?

Botox Cosmetic (onabotulinumtoxin A) has been FDA approved since 2002, and provides temporary improvement in frown lines and wrinkles around the eyes. Millions of patients have been treated with Botox Cosmetic and there are extensive data establishing safety. This purified protein works by weakening muscle contraction to soften overlying lines and wrinkles for several months.

The FDA also has approved injectable dermal fillers for deep facial wrinkles and folds. These include Restylane, Perlane, Juvederm, Radiesse, Belotero Balance, and Voluma. New additional neuromodulators similar to Botox Cosmetic are also available: Xeomin (incobotulinumtoxin A) and Dysport (abobotulinumtoxin A).
