CHAPTER GOALS

  • Identify the structures of the lymphatic and immune systems and understand how the systems work. 
  • Learn basic terminology, combining forms, and other word parts related to these systems. 
  • Recognize terms describing pathologic conditions involving components of the lymphatic and immune systems. 
  • Identify laboratory tests, clinical procedures, and abbreviations relating to the lymphatic and immune systems. 
  • Apply your new knowledge to understanding medical terms in their proper contexts, such as medical reports and records.
