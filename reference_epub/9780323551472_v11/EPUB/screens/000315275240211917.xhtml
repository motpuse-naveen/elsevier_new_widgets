Each exercise is designed not as a test, but rather as an opportunity for you to learn the material.

N, Part 2 Essay Question Group

Define the term.

Define the term.

Question 1

1.

cerebrovascular accident 

Feedback

A cerebrovascular accident, or stroke, is damage to the blood vessels of the cerebrum, leading to death of brain cells.

Question 2

2.

encephalitis

Feedback

Encephalitis is inflammation of the brain.

Question 3

3.

cystoscope

Feedback

A cystoscope is an instrument used to visually examine the urinary bladder. The cystoscope is inserted into the urethra and urinary bladder.

Question 4

4.

transhepatic

Feedback

Transhepatic means pertaining to across or through the liver.

Question 5

5.

osteogenic sarcoma 

Feedback

Osteogenic sarcoma is a malignant (cancerous) tumor originating in bone, which is considered a fleshy (connective) tissue of the body.

Question 6

6.

hypogastric

Feedback

Hypogastric means pertaining to below the stomach.

Question 7

7.

endocrine glands 

Feedback

Endocrine glands secrete hormones within the body. Examples of these are the pituitary, thyroid, and adrenal glands.

Question 8

8.

nephrectomy

Feedback

Nephrectomy is the removal of a kidney.

Question 9

9.

exocrine glands 

Feedback

Exocrine glands secrete chemicals to the outside of the body. Examples are the sweat, lacrimal or tear-producing, prostate, and salivary glands.

Question 10

10.

neuralgia

Feedback

Neuralgia is nerve pain.
