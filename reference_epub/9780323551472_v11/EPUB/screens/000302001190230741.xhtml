Encephal/o, Cerebr/o, Cephal/o, Crani/o, and Psych/o

Don’t confuse the meanings of these combining forms!

Encephal/o = brain
Cerebr/o = cerebrum (largest part of the brain)
Cephal/o = head
Crani/o = skull
Psych/o = mind

 Larynx and Other Parts of the Body Ending in x

coccyx = tailbone
larynx = voice box
pharynx = throat
phalanx = finger or toe

To make combining forms for parts of the body that end in x, substitute g for x:

laryng/o
