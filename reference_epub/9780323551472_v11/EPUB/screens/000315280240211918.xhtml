J Essay Question Group

Question 1

Explain what happens in a fecal transplant and why it is necessary.

Feedback

In a fecal transplant, stool from a healthy donor, is transferred into the colon of a patient/recipient. This is necessary when the patient’s colon contains harmful bacteria, such as C. difficile. After transplant, normal stool bacteria populate the colon of the patient.
